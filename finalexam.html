Final Exam Information


STA130 Final Exam Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is the exam?

See the University of Toronto’s Faculty of Arts and Science April 2018 Examination Timetable.

What are the rules for writing an exam at UofT?

See the University of Toronto’s Exam Rule Reminder

What content will be covered on the final exam?

The final exam will include all the material covered in the course. The material covered in the course can be found on the course content page.

What should I study?

The exam will cover all the material before and after the midterm test. So, study:

  • lecture notes,

  • weekly practice problems,

  • midterm tests,

  • mideterm test review, and

  • final exam sample questions.

How many questions?

There are 23 multiple choice questions, and one writing question.

How much is each question worth?

The value of each question is given on the April 2018 exam cover page.

Should I study previous STA130 final exams?

No. The course content has substantially changed from previous years.

Are there any sample questions available?

Yes. Professors Taback and Gibbs have created sample questions available here. Answers to these questions will be posted later.
